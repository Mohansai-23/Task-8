Working with VPN's:

* Encrypt data traffic between your device and the VPN server using protocols like OpenVPN, WireGuard, or IKEv2.

* Prevent the local network from seeing your browsing activity.

* Hides IP address from websites.


VPN Benefits:
* Encrypts internet traffic for privacy and security.

* Hides your IP address, enhancing anonymity.

* Allows access to geo-restricted content.

* Protects data on public Wi-Fi.

VPN Limitations:
* May slow down browsing speed due to encryption overhead.

* Free VPNs often have data, speed, and server limitations.

* Does not prevent phishing or malware attacks.
